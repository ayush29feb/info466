About Us

Our team has been centered around the theme “Day in the Life.” By connecting our properties through this storyline, we were able to create a cohesive story while also keeping the class engaged through our teleportation balls. Continuing our theme, we toured Fleur as students “studying abroad,” showing students what it is like to be immersed in the peaceful environment and engaging with the interactive devices at our location.
